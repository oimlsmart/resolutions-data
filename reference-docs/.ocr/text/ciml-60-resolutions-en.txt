Resolution CIML/2025/10                                                               Agenda item 6.4
The Committee,
Noting the information provided by the BIML Director on Member State and Corresponding Member
classes and fees,
Recalling Resolutions CIML/2021/13 of the 56th CIML Meeting, and Conference/2021/07 of the 16th

Resolution CIML/2025/21                                                             Agenda item 7.3
The Committee,
Noting the information in CIML Meeting Document 7.3,
Appreciating the contributions made by CIML Members of Honour to the work of the Committee,
Approves the withdrawal of OIML B 17:2012 Policies and rules for the reimbursement of travel

Amendment 1 dated 2023-11), and

by-case basis under the general financial and travel policies of the BIML.

Resolution CIML/2025/22                                                                 Agenda item 7.4
The Committee,
Notes the importance of the OIML Bulletin in disseminating updates on legal metrology,
Welcomes the online, open-access nature of the OIML Bulletin,
Welcomes the introduction of individual article numbering,
Encourages the BIML to continue efforts to improve access to the contents,
Welcomes the input of Guest Editors in identifying and attracting contributions on key topics of interest,
Encourages Members to propose key topics and to nominate appropriate Guest Editors for future
editions,
Thanks those individuals who have been Mentors of recent editions, those who have already offered to
be Guest Editors of future editions, and all the contributing authors, and
Confirms its continued support for the OIML Bulletin.


Resolution CIML/2025/23                                                                 Agenda item 7.4
The Committee,
Notes the growing importance of digital identifiers across all domains,
Encourages the use by the legal metrology community of digital identifiers such as Digital Object
Identifiers (DOIs), Research Organization Registry identifiers (RORs) and Open Researcher and
Contributor IDs (ORCiDs),
Welcomes the introduction into the OIML Bulletin of RORs and ORCiDs,
Encourages Members publishing in the OIML Bulletin to register for an ORCiD,
Encourages Members to maintain their institute’s ROR record up to date,
Welcomes the efforts of the BIML to register DOIs for OIML Recommendations and other OIML
publications,
Encourages the BIML to register DOIs for articles in the OIML Bulletin, and
Thanks the BIML Coordinator for her work.
