Resolution no. CIML/2024/37                                                         Agenda item 18.4
The Committee,
Recognising the importance of issues in the area of climate change to society, and to the activities of
the OIML,
Considering that a number of the OIML’s current activities and technical work projects are aimed at
better regulation and cooperation in the field of climate change in legal metrology activities,
Requests the BIML to complete the procedures necessary for the OIML to become an “accepted
observer” in the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC),
Requests the BIML to continue to engage with other stakeholders in the field of climate change, and
Encourages its Members to promote the use of legal metrology as an important tool in climate change
mitigation and in the energy transition.
