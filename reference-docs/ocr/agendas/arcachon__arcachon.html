  Introduction

With its exceptional architecture and bustling town centre all year round overlooking miles of beach, Arcachon has it all. Each quarter exudes its own unique atmosphere and charm: elegant 19th century villas in the Winter Town, sea front and piers in the Summer Town and a lively port in the Autumn Town are the three most known.

The Spring Town, built around Saint Anne-des-Abatilles Spring, looks onto the long Pereire beach and its pedestrian promenades, while charming "le Moulleau" is a veritable village within the town.

 

  Venue - Palais des Congrès

The meeting venue, Palais des Congrès (or in English the Arcachon Congress Center) faces Arcachon Bay and consists of 1300m² of areas for holding all types of events, from a meeting for 10 people to a congress with 700 participants.

Each year, approximately 200 conferences, seminars, regional, national and international events choose to come to Arcachon: the perfect destination for combining utility with pleasure.

The Center consists of a 500-seat auditorium, modular meeting rooms with direct views over Arcachon Bay, exhibition rooms, and the Olympia Theatre.
