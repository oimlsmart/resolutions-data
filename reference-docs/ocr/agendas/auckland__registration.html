Registration for the 49th CIML Meeting and Seminar

  CIML Members

  OIML Corresponding Members


All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are required to register online no later than 15 July 2014.

By clicking the button below, a new window will open and you will be directed to the secure Members area of the main OIML website. Please login and you will be taken to the registration page.

 


To view the summary of those Member States and Corresponding Members that have so far registered for the CIML Meeting, please click the button below:

 

  Observers


Liaison Organizations and Countries that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members may attend the 49th CIML Meeting as Observers (space permitting).

Please download the form below to register as an Observer, type in your information in the fields provided, then fax or e-mail it to Mrs. Patricia Saint-Germain, Administrator, Members, at the BIML no later than 15 July 2014. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
