46th CIML Meeting and Prague CTT Seminar registration

To view the summary of those Member States and Corresponding Members that have so far registered for the CIML Meeting, click the button below:


REGISTRATION PROCEDURE:


1) CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members

All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are requested to register (or designate a proxy) for the 46th CIML Meeting using the online form below no later than 30 June 2011. To begin the registration process, please click the button below. You may also register online for the CTT Seminar.

IMPORTANT:

- On the first screen enter the general login (members) and the associated password, and
- On the second screen enter your country's two-digit ISO code followed by your personal Member password.


2) Observers

Liaison Organizations and Countries that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members may attend the CIML Meeting and the CTT Seminar as Observers (space permitting).

Please download the form below to register as an Observer at the 46th CIML Meeting and CTT Seminar, type in your information in the fields provided, then fax or e-mail it to the BIML no later than 30 June 2011. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
