Seminar on the OIML Website



Agenda

 

The objective of the Seminar is to review the OIML website and demonstrate the additional modules that have been developed since the 2013 CIML meeting. The first session will be in the form of a presentation and the second session will be in the form of a live demonstration in which Members may participate using their own laptops.

 

 

Session 1

 

1.1  Introduction

1.2  Review of the system hardware/firmware

1.3  Review of the database

1.4  Review of the website design

 

 

Session 2

 

2.1  Demonstration of the CIML Member module

2.2  Demonstration of the Corresponding Member module

2.3  Demonstration of the Project Group management module

 

Questions and comments (time permitting)
