Visa information and entry requirements


 

  Visa information for Germany

 

Generally speaking, all non-EU nationals require a visa for stays in Germany. A visa is not required for visits of up to 90 days in a 180‑day period for nationals of those countries for which the European Community has abolished the visa requirement. You will find an overview of those countries here.

If you do require a visa, you may only obtain it at your local German Embassy or Consulate. A list of all German representations with their contact information can be found here. For individual requirements (paperwork, fees) to obtain a visa please also check this link.
