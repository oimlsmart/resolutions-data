Round Table on RLMOs

A Round Table on Regional Legal Metrology Organizations (RLMOs) will take place on Monday 27 October 2008 from 14:00 to 17:00 at the Star City Hotel (Pirrama Room). Attendance is restricted to members of RLMOs and Liaison Organizations.
