  Visa information for the Slovak Republic

 

Generally speaking, all non-EU nationals require a visa for stays in the Slovak Republic. Useful information on Schengen visas can be found here*.

If you do require a visa, a list of Diplomatic Missions in Slovakia and Slovak Diplomatic Missions abroad can be found here*.

The Slovak Office of Standards, Metrology and Testing can assist with visa enquiries, please see the Contact page for details. To support your visa application, you may also download the official invitation letter from the Slovak Office of Standards, Metrology and Testing:

.
