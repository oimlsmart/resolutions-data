Registration procedure

All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are requested to please register electronically via this web site for the 42nd CIML Meeting and for the Seminar on OIML D 1.

To proceed, please click the button below and (after entering the general Members' login and password) enter your country's two-digit ISO code followed by your personal CIML Member login.

You may amend your Country's list of Delegates at any time in the future if required by logging into this page again.
