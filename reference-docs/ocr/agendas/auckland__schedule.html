Schedule and arrangements


  Registration

The Registration Desk for both the Seminar and the 49th CIML Meeting will be open outside the main meeting room in the SkyCity Auckland Convention Center on:

  • Sunday 2 November from 16:00 to 19:00.

The Registration Desk for the 49th CIML Meeting will be open outside the main meeting room in the SkyCity Auckland Convention Center on:

  • Monday 3 November from 16:00 to 19:00 and
  • Tuesday 4 November from 08:30 to 12:00.
  

  Meals and breaks

Meal arrangements are as follows:

  • Morning refreshments and lunch will be served on Monday through Thursday;
  • Afternoon refreshments will be served on Tuesday and Wednesday.

  Group photograph

The group photograph will be taken on Tuesday 4 November before lunch.
