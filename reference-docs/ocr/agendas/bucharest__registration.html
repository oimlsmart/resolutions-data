Registration for the 14th Conference and 47th CIML Meeting

CIML Members and
OIML Corresponding Members

Registration for the 14th Conference and for the 47th CIML Meeting will be online via this page. All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are requested to register using the online form below no later than 1 July 2012.

According to the OIML Convention, no proxies are allowed for the Conference. Please also note that for the Conference (OIML Member States only) a written confirmation is required, duly signed, stating the names, titles and email addresses of the three official delegates, and indicating the head of the delegation. This confirmation is to be sent to the BIML by mail, fax or email attachment.


Observers

Liaison Organizations and Countries that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members may attend the Conference and CIML Meeting as Observers (space permitting).

Please download the form below to register as an Observer at the 14th Conference and 47th CIML Meeting, type in your information in the fields provided, then fax or e-mail it to the BIML no later than 1 July 2012. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
