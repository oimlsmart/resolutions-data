  Bratislava

Bratislava is located in southwestern Slovakia, occupying both banks of the River Danube and the left bank of the River Morava, and bordering Austria and Hungary.

The city is the political, cultural and economic centre of Slovakia. It is the seat of the Slovak president, the parliament and the Slovak Executive. It has several universities, and many museums, theatres, galleries and other cultural and educational institutions. Many of Slovakia's large businesses and financial institutions have headquarters there.


  Worth knowing

- Population: 430 000 inhabitants
- Size: 367 km2
- 18 km from the border with Hungary
- 60 km from Vienna

Attached to Bratislava’s new Ondrej Nepela sports complex, this modern 4-star hotel offers free WiFi and free wellness facilities with a heated indoor pool, 2 saunas and a fitness center available 24/7.

The spacious rooms at the DoubleTree Bratislava are air-conditioned and soundproof. They feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a laptop-size safe, ironing facilities and a mini-bar. Free toiletries are provided in the bathrooms which also come with a hairdryer.

The Doubletree is situated 10 minutes drive from Bratislava’s historic city centre, seven minutes from Bratislava (BTS) Airport and only 50 minutes from Vienna (VIE) International Airport. The DoubleTree is a convenient location to link by road and rail to Hungary, Austria, the Czech Republic, Germany and Poland. Total function space is 1,200 m2.
