Registration for the 48th CIML Meeting

CIML Members and
OIML Corresponding Members


Registration for the 48th CIML Meeting will be online via this page. All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are requested to register using the online form below no later than 1 July 2013.

Observers
 


Liaison Organizations and Countries that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members may attend the 48th CIML Meeting as Observers (space permitting).

Please download the form below to register as an Observer, type in your information in the fields provided, then fax or e-mail it to Mrs. Patricia Saint-Germain, Administrator, Members, at the BIML no later than 1 July 2013. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
