Registration for the e-Learning Workshop and the 54th CIML Meeting

  CIML Members

  OIML Corresponding Members


All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are required to register online no later than 1 July 2019.

By clicking the button below, a new window will open and you will be directed to the secure My Access area of the main OIML website.

Note: If you are not already logged into the main OIML website you will need to do so before you can access the registration page.

IMPORTANT: The BIML kindly requests all CIML Members to provide at least one proxy in the event that you are unable to attend the 54th CIML Meeting.


To view the list of Members that have registered to date, click the button below.

  Observers


Liaison Organizations and Countries or Economies that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members may attend the e-Learning Workshop and the 54th CIML Meeting as Observers (space permitting).

Please download the form below to register as an Observer, type in your information in the fields provided, then fax or e-mail it to Mrs. Patricia Saint-Germain, Administrator, Members, at the BIML no later than 1 July 2019. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
