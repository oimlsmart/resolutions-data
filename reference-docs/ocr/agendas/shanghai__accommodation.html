Okura Garden Hotel (CIML Meeting venue)

Rating: 5 *****
Address: 58 Maoming Nan Lu, Shanghai 200020  Tel.: +86 21-6415-1111 Fax: +86 21-6415-8866

The CIML and associated meetings will be held in the Okura Garden Hotel, a 492-room 1920's French Art Deco style luxury hotel offering Standard, Executive, Deluxe Rooms and also a number of suites.

The hotel is situated in the tranquil grounds of a 28 000 m2 garden and offers a large number of facilities including Chinese, Japanese, French and Teppanyaki restaurants, coffee shops, several wine and cocktail lounges, bars, a 25 m pool, health club, shops and business center.

The CIML Meeting will be held in the Grand Ballroom, once the most prestigious ballroom in Shanghai (see photo below). Visit the Okura Garden Hotel web site for more information.

Jin Jiang Hotel

Rating: 5 *****
Address: 59, Mao Ming Road South, 200020 Shanghai. Tel.: +86 21-3218-9888 Fax: +86 21-6472-5588
Location: Shanxi Road subway station (Line 1), directly opposite the Okura Garden Hotel

An historic hotel, the Jin Jiang Hotel Shanghai has served more than 400 heads of state and government leaders over the past 70 years. One of the best-located hotels in Shanghai, it is conveniently situated close to Huaihai Road, the "Champs-Elysées" of Shanghai.

Visit the Jin Jiang Hotel web site for more information.

City Hotel

Rating: 4 ****
Address: 5-7 Shanxi Road (South), Shanghai. Tel.: +86 21-6255-1133 Fax: +86 21-6255-0211
Location: Short walking distance from the Okura Garden Hotel

Four-star hotel located in the heart of downtown Shanghai, next to the Shanghai Exhibition Center. Visit the City Hotel web site for more information.
