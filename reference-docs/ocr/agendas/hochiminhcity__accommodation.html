The Sheraton Saigon Hotel & Towers

Located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City’s vibrant business and entertainment district, the 5* Sheraton Saigon Hotel & Towers is a conveniently situated luxury hotel.

The hotel has 358 spacious guest rooms, 112 studios and a number of suites, all with personalized service and amenities. It boasts eight stylish restaurants and bars, and the highly acclaimed outdoor Level 23 Wine Bar is an unforgettable place to watch a stunning sunset over the city while enjoying a leisurely drink.

The 17 meeting rooms (covering a total of 2 500 m2) and the convention facilities can cater for up to 1 200 guests. The Grand Ballroom is the largest pillar-less hotel function room in Ho Chi Minh City, and is a huge 720 m2 in size, with a 5 m high ceiling.

Equipped with a swimming pool, state-of-the-art fitness center and spa, the Sheraton Saigon Hotel has excellent facilities. Nearby are the Opera House and Reunification Palace and bargains can always be found at the Ben Thanh markets.


Special negotiated Deluxe Room rate

By booking strictly before 6 September 2013 (one month before the event), OIML delegates may benefit from the special negotiated Deluxe Room rate of 123 € (single) or 138 € (double/twin) per night. This special rate includes taxes, VAT and daily buffet breakfast, wi-fi internet, two bottles of mineral water per day, and free access to the swimming pool, fitness center, sauna and steam room.

All individual guests are responsible for booking their own rooms, and for settling their own accounts on the day of departure by cash or credit card. Personal cheques will not be accepted. On arrival a pre-authorization will be taken on the client's credit card. A credit card number is also required to book the room. Please note that the Hotel has advised us that an additional charge equivalent to 3 % of the total bill will be made when payment is made by credit card.
