Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table


A Regional Legal Metrology Organization (RLMO) Round Table meeting
will take place on Monday 9 October 2017 from 15:00 to 16:30
at the Las Américas International Convention and Exhibition Center.

Please note that attendance is restricted to delegates
who have been registered by invited organizations.

 

Letter from the Convener:

 

1    Welcome by the convener
2 Roll call
3 Confirmation of the agenda
4 Updates from the regional organizations
The regional organizations are invited to give a brief presentation of their activities during the past year.
5 OIML-CS and the RLMOs
The OIML-CS will start on January 1, 2018.  New ideas and possible actions will be discussed to help the new certification system start and develop smoothly.
6

Sharable documents and materials among RLMOs on the RLMO RT website
At the last meeting sharing e-learning videos was discussed.  We will continue the discussion.  Comments and opinions to improve the newly constructed website will be also invited.
7 Other matters
8 Conclusions
The convener will report on the conclusions of this Round Table at the 52nd CIML Meeting, which will be held over the following days.
9 Arrangements for the next RLMO Round Table and closure of the meeting
