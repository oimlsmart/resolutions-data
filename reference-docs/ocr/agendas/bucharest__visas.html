Visa information

Official Romanian visa information

A valid passport is required by all individuals wishing to enter Romania. A visa may also be necessary; detailed information on how to apply for and obtain the appropriate visa is available on the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs web site. Delegates are advised to leave enough time to apply for and obtain the appropriate visa, without which entry into Romania may not be granted at the airport or border check point.
