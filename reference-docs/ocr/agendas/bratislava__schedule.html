Schedule and arrangements


  Registration

The Registration Desk will be open outside the main meeting room in the Hotel Doubletree by Hilton on:

  • Sunday 20 October from 16:00 to 19:00
  • Monday 21 October from 16:00 to 19:00
  • Tuesday 22 October from 08:30 to 12:00
  

  Meals and breaks

Meal arrangements are as follows:

  • Morning refreshments will be served on Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday;
  • Lunch will be served on Monday through Thursday;
  • Afternoon refreshments will be served on Monday and Tuesday.

  Group photograph

The group photograph will be taken on Friday 25 October during the coffee break.
