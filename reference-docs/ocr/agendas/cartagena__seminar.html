Seminar on the OIML Certification System (OIML-CS)

 

The new OIML Certification System (OIML-CS) is due to become effective on 01 January 2018. The key emphasis of this seminar is to provide information on the implementation of the OIML-CS, including the steps that potential OIML Issuing Authorities, Utilizers and Associates will need to take to join the OIML-CS.

The seminar is open to representatives of OIML Member States and Corresponding Member countries, representatives from RLMOs, potential OIML Issuing Authorities, Utilizers and Associates, manufacturer representatives and others who are interested in the OIML-CS. It will take place on Monday 9 October 2017 from 08:30 to 14:30 at the Las Américas International Convention and Exhibition Center.
