Note concerning proxies

CIML Members who are unable to attend the 57th CIML Meeting may give a proxy in line with the following:

  1. Proxies may only be given after registration closes, i.e. from 30 September 2022 at 15:00 UTC until Friday 7 October 2022 at 15:00 UTC.
  2. CIML Members may give ONE proxy.
  3. Proxies may only be given to registered CIML Members.
  4. In addition to their own vote, a registered CIML Member may only receive a maximum of TWO proxies.
  5. Before giving a proxy to a registered CIML Member, please obtain their agreement.
  6. Proxies are given for the duration of the 57th CIML Meeting.
