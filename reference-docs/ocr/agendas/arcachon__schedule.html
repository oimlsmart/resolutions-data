Schedule and arrangements


  Registration

The Registration Desk for both the CEEMS Seminar and the 50th CIML Meeting will be open outside the main meeting room in the Palais des Congrès on:

  • Sunday 18 October from 16:00 to 19:00.

The Registration Desk for the 50th CIML Meeting will be open outside the main meeting room in the Palais des Congrès on:

  • Monday 19 October from 08:30 to 19:00 and
  • Tuesday 20 October from 08:30 to 12:00.
  

  Meals and breaks

Meal arrangements are as follows:

  • Morning refreshments will be served on Monday through Thursday;
  • Lunch will be served on Monday through Wednesday;
  • Afternoon refreshments will be served on Monday through Wednesday.

  Group photograph

The group photograph will be taken on Tuesday 20 October before lunch.
