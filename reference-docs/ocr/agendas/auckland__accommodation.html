Important update 2014-10-01

As we are now within 30 days of the accommodation bookings for CIML delegates at the SkyCity Hotel, any unsold rooms in the block booking have been released. Therefore, delegates can no longer make bookings through the direct link previously published on this website.

Delegates may still book accommodation at the SkyCity Hotel by contacting SkyCity Hotel directly (E-mail or +64 9 363 6000) and by advising that they are part of the CIML/OIML group.

Any further room requests will be subject to availability and best available rate at the time of booking.



  SkyCity Hotel, Auckland

The SkyCity Hotel is located adjacent to the SkyCity Convention Centre (the Meeting venue) with access to the Convention Centre via an air bridge.  The SkyCity Hotel is part of the SkyCity precinct which includes the Casino and Sky Tower.  Over ten restaurants and bars are located within a short walk and the central business district is a five-minute walk away. The hotel has 323 recently refurbished rooms, all of which have complimentary wifi (with no data cap or time limit), LCD television, air-conditioning, tea and coffee making facilities and a desk. Check-in is from 3pm and checkout up to 11am.
