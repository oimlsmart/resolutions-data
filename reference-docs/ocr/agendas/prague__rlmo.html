Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table


A Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table meeting took place
on Tuesday 11 October 2011 (09:00-12:30) at the Clarion Congress Hotel.

Attendance was restricted to delegates who have been registered by invited organizations.

The meeting was chaired by Dr. Pavel Neyezhmakov.
For detailed information on the agenda, please use the contact details below.

The presentations given may be downloaded here: RLMOs WorkGroup Web Site

Draft Agenda

1 Welcome by the Chair
2 Roll call
3 Confirmation of the agenda
4 Terms of Reference of the Round Table
5 Revised OIML Strategy and relationship with the RLMOs
6 Brief updates from Regional Organizations
7 Possible common actions
8 Information on training
9 Information on developing country aspects
10 Other matters
11   Draft conclusions
