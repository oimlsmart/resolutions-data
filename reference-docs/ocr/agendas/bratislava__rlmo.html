Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table


A Regional Legal Metrology Organization (RLMO) Round Table meeting
will take place on Tuesday 22 October 2019 from 09:00 to 11:30
at the Hotel Doubletree by Hilton.

Please note that attendance is restricted to delegates
who have been registered by invited organizations.
