5. Others

There are several other hotels close to the Radisson Blu Hotel, but special rates have not been negotiated. Hotel standards in the center of Hamburg are generally high and only vary in detail (including/excluding breakfast, wifi, etc.) and price. While making your choice, however, please bear in mind that we will not be able to provide a shuttle service to the Radisson Blu Hotel and you will therefore have to walk or use public transportation. Possible options close-by are:

  • Grand Elysée Hamburg
