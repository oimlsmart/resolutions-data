Online 55th CIML Meeting: Practical information

 

Please check this page frequently
as the information will be updated on a regular basis.
Last updated: 2020-10-12

 

  Languages

The CIML meeting will be conducted in English. Simultaneous interpretation into and from French will be provided via the online meeting platform.


  Meeting documents

The CIML meeting documents are available for download via the CIML Meeting Agenda page.


  Online video conference platform - Guide for Participants - Guide for Presenters - Test runs

a) Platform

The platform that will be used for the online 55th CIML meeting is Zoom.



b) Test runs

Two test runs will take place in advance of the online 55th CIML Meeting on the following dates:

Test run 1: Thursday, 1 October 2020, 10:00–12:00 UTC
Test run 2: Monday, 19 October 2020, 10:00–11:00 UTC

The objectives of the test runs are to test your connectivity and use of the Zoom functions, for the BIML to answer any questions concerning the use of Zoom, and to resolve any potential difficulties. The agenda for these test runs can be downloaded here:

(Version 1 dated 2020-09-28)

  Registration

All delegates intending to participate in the online 55th CIML Meeting are required to register online (see the Registration page) at their earliest possible convenience, but in any case no later than 2 October 2020.


  Time zone

The online 55th CIML Meeting will take place from 10:00 to 13:00 UTC (with one short break) to enable all of our Members to participate. Local time in all major cities in the world can be found on the World Time and Date website:
