Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table


A Regional Legal Metrology Organization (RLMO) Round Table meeting
will take place on Monday 19 October 2015 from 17:30 at the
Palais des Congrès.

Please note that attendance is restricted to delegates who have been
registered by invited organizations.

 

Letter from the Convener:

 

 

Agenda

 

1    Welcome by the convener
2 Roll call
3 Confirmation of the agenda
4 Updates from the regional organizations
The regional organizations are invited to give a brief presentation of their activities during the past year.
5 Possible common actions
6 New web pages for the RLMO Round Table on the OIML website
In previous Round Table discussions, it was suggested that better use could be made of the OIML website in the communication between the participants. Now that the OIML website is being reconstructed, it is a good opportunity to discuss possible functionalities that could be made available on the RLMO web pages.
7 Other matters
8 Conclusions
The convener will report the conclusions of this Round Table at the 50th CIML Meeting, which will be held on the following days.
9 Arrangements for a next RLMO Round Table and closure of the meeting
