Online 55th CIML Meeting: Schedule

 

The online 55th CIML Meeting will take place on the Zoom meeting platform on the days indicated below. There will be a three-hour session each day, with one short break.

Please connect to the Zoom meeting at least 30–60 minutes before it begins (especially if you do not have experience logging into and using Zoom) to allow time to address any potential connection problems and to ensure that you are connected before the discussions begin.

The BIML has prepared a Guide for Participants which explains the various Zoom platform functions and the meeting protocol.

 

Meeting schedule:

  Tuesday 20 October 2020

10:00 - 13:00 UTC

  Wednesday 21 October 2020

10:00 - 13:00 UTC

  Thursday 22 October 2020

10:00 - 13:00 UTC
