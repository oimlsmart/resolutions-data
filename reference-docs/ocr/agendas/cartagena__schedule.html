Schedule and arrangements


  Registration

The Registration Desk will be open outside the main meeting room in the Las Américas International Convention and Exhibition Center on:

  • Sunday 08 October from 16:00 to 19:00
    (OIML-CS Seminar and 52nd CIML Meeting)
  • Monday 09 October from 08:00 to 17:30
    (OIML-CS Seminar and 52nd CIML Meeting)
  • Tuesday 10 October from 08:00 to 17:30
    (52nd CIML Meeting).

Badges will be provided for the CIML only.

  

  Meals and breaks

Meal arrangements are as follows:

  • Morning refreshments will be served on Monday through Thursday;
  • Lunch will be served on Monday through Thursday;
  • Afternoon refreshments will be served on Monday through Wednesday.

  Group photograph

The group photograph will be taken on Tuesday 10 October before lunch.
