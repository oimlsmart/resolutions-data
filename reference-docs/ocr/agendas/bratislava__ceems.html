CEEMS Advisory Group meeting


The CEEMS Advisory Group (AG) meeting
will take place on Monday 21 October 2019 from 15:00 to 17:30
at the Hotel Doubletree by Hilton, Bratislava.

Please note that attendance is restricted to members of the CEEMS AG
(see OIML B 19:2017 Terms of Reference for the Advisory Group on matters concerning
Countries and Economies with Emerging Metrology Systems (CEEMS) for more information).

 

Letter from the CEEMS AG Chairperson:

 

Draft Agenda (v1, 2019-04-08)

 

1    Welcome, changes in AG membership, proxies
2 Minutes of the last meeting
3 Report on the OIML Pilot Training Centers (OPTC)
4 Future strategy for Training Centers (framework) and branding of CEEMS
5 Update on the website (including expert database and survey)
6 e-learning modules
7 Possible scholarship program for future leaders in CEEMS
8 Revision of D 1, D 14 and D 19
9 Study on Conformity to Type problems
10 Report from the BIPM on its CEEMS activities
11 Proposal for cooperation with BIPM CBKT
12 Development of the AG work plan
13 Report to the CIML on the AG’s activities
