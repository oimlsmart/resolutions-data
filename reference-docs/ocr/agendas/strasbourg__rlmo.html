Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table


A Regional Legal Metrology Organization (RLMO) Round Table meeting
will take place on Monday 17 October 2016 from 09:00 to 12:30 at the
Convention and Exhibition Center.

Please note that attendance is restricted to delegates who have been
registered by invited organizations.

 

Letter from the Convener:

 

 

Agenda

 

1    Welcome by the convener
2 Roll call
3 Confirmation of the agenda
4 Updates from the Regional Organizations and Observers
The regional organizations are invited to give a brief presentation of their activities during the past year. The invited Observers will also give brief presentations.
5 Discussion with the Advisory Group CEEMS
The representative of the Advisory Group CEEMS will join us and give a brief report on their activities. We will then discuss possible cooperation.
6 Possible common actions
7 New provisional web pages for the RLMO Round Table
In previous Round Table discussions, it was suggested that better use could be made of the OIML website in the communication between the participants. A provisional OIML-RT website has been constructed, and comments and opinions to improve the site, which will be integrated into
the OIML website, are expected at the meeting.
8 Other matters
9 Conclusions
The convener will report the conclusions of this Round Table at the 51st CIML Meeting, which will be held over the following days.
10 Arrangements for the next RLMO Round Table and closure of the meeting
