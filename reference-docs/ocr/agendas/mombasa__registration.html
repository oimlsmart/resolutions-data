44th CIML Meeting and OIML Seminar registration

Introduction

The OIML Seminar "Stakes and priorities of legal metrology for trade" is open to all delegates who have registered to attend the 44th CIML Meeting (Member States, Corresponding Members, Liaisons and Observers, see below) and to representatives of institutions or companies interested in international trade (associations of producers and exporters of goods and commodities, associations of consumers, etc.), at international, regional or national level.

There is no participation fee; however, the number of places is strictly limited, so we cannot guarantee that all applicants can be registered. The registration procedures are detailed below.

1) CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members

All CIML Members and OIML Corresponding Members are requested to register (or designate a proxy) for the 44th CIML Meeting and the OIML Seminar using the online form below. To begin the registration process, please click the button below and enter your country's two-digit ISO code followed by your personal CIML Member password.


2) Observers

- Liaison Organizations
  and
- Countries that are neither OIML Member States nor Corresponding Members

may attend the OIML Seminar and may also attend the CIML Meeting as Observers (space permitting).

- Persons representing organizations or economic sectors interested in the stakes of legal metrology

may attend the OIML Seminar (space permitting).


Please download the PDF form below to register as an Observer at the 44th CIML Meeting and OIML Seminar and fax or e-mail it to the BIML no later than 15 July 2009. Space will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis and attendees will be advised of the status of their application.
