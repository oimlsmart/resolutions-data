14th International Conference and
47th CIML Meeting
 2012 

Bucharest, Romania
 

The 14th International Conference on Legal Metrology and the
47th Meeting of the International Committee of Legal Metrology
took place at the Radisson Blu Hotel, Bucharest, Romania
from Monday 1 through Friday 5 October 2012.

A Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table
also took place during the week.
