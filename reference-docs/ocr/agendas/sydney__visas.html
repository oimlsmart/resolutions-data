Visa Information

All travelers to Australia must have a valid visa before boarding their plane; they cannot apply for a visa on arrival. (NB: The only exceptions are New Zealand and Norfolk Island passport holders who will be issued a visa on arrival in Australia and who normally do not need to lodge a visa application prior to their departure, unless there are character or health concerns).

Participants require either:

* a Subclass 977 - Business (Short Validity) ETA (Electronic Travel Authority) Visa - only passport holders of the countries listed in the table below are eligible;

or

* a Subclass 456 - Business (Short Stay) Visa - for those who are not eligible for an ETA visa.

These visas are intended for visits of up to three months.
