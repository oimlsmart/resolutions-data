Schedule of Meetings

Schedule:    

Immediately prior to the CTT Seminar, a short Seminar, organized by OIML TC 3/SC 4, will be held on Monday morning 10 October from 09:00 to 10:30. The theme is "Application of statistical methods in metrological control" and those wishing to attend are invited to contact Susanne Ludwig directly (on behalf of the TC 3/SC 4 Secretariat). Please note that the BIML is not involved in the organization of this Seminar.

The Registration Desk for the CTT Seminar (which will start at 11:00 am) will be open in the Clarion Congress Hotel:

  • on Sunday 09 October from 16:00 to 19:00;
  • on Monday 10 October from 08:30 to 10:00.

The Registration Desk for the 46th CIML Meeting will be open in the Clarion Congress Hotel:

  • on Sunday 09 October from 16:00 to 19:00;
  • on Monday 10 October from 08:30 to 10:00 and from 16:00 to 19:00;
  • on Tuesday 11 October from 08:30 to 11:30.

Lunch will be served on Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday. Morning refreshments will be served Monday through Friday, and afternoon refreshments will be served Monday through Wednesday.
