15th International Conference
51st CIML Meeting
17-21 October 2016
Strasbourg, France

The 15th International Conference on Legal Metrology and the
51st Meeting of the International Committee of Legal Metrology
will take place at the Convention and Exhibition Center
(Palais de la Musique et des Congrès), Strasbourg, France
from Monday 17 through Friday 21 October 2016.

A Regional Legal Metrology Organization Round Table will also take place during the week.
