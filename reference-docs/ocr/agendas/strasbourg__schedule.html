Schedule and arrangements


  Registration

The Registration Desk for both the 15th Conference and the 51st CIML Meeting will be open outside the main meeting room in the Convention and Exhibition Center on:

  • Sunday 16 October from 16:00 to 19:00,
  • Monday 17 October from 08:00 to 17:30, and
  • Tuesday 18 October from 08:00 to 17:30.
  

  Meals and breaks

Meal arrangements are as follows:

  • Morning refreshments will be served on Tuesday through Friday;
  • Lunch will be served on Monday through Friday;
  • Afternoon refreshments will be served on Monday, Tuesday and Friday.

  Group photograph

The group photograph will be taken on Wednesday 19 October before lunch.
